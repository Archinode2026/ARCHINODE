## 3. Sending rules (protects Silvia's inbox reputation)

**Volume**
- **Max 5 emails per day** for the first week. Ramp up only after seeing that no bounces or spam complaints have come back.
- Spread across days, not all at 9am. Morning + late afternoon batches work well.

**Which template to use**
- Default: **Template A** for first contact
- **Template C** for brands you know are actively exporting or looking at Asia (research 60 seconds — LinkedIn "About" section usually tells)
- Send from **silviavandone@hotmail.com** (already verified in email signature)

**Personalization — non-negotiable**
- Every email must fill in: `[First Name]`, `[Brand Name]`, `[specific product line]` (one sentence about their actual work — check their site)
- Skipping this drops reply rate by ~70%. Not optional.

**Tracking**
- After each send, update the xlsx:
  - Column J `First Contact Date` — YYYY-MM-DD
  - Column K `Response` — leave blank until they reply
- When a reply arrives:
  - Positive → K = "Interested, call scheduled" or similar
  - Negative → K = brief reason ("Not exporting Asia now" / "Already have Korean partner")
- 7~10 days after Template A with no reply → send Template B (same thread, "Re: [original subject]")
